About Uncanny Cat Golf

Uncanny Cat Golf explores how par, strokes, time, and level information keep the round readable while the scenery tries to steal attention. The joke is immediate because the ball is a square cat and the course looks assembled from several incompatible corners of the internet, but the underlying challenge remains precise. Every stroke still has a starting position, direction, power level, rebound, stopping point, and cost on the scorecard. Uncanny Cat Golf FAQ

What is Uncanny Cat Golf?▾
Uncanny Cat Golf is a score-driven browser mini-golf game where a square cat replaces the ball and bizarre collage-style courses surround familiar aim, power, hazard, and par rules.
Can I play Uncanny Cat Golf online?▾
Yes. Press Play above to launch the embedded browser build without installing a separate desktop package.
How do I control the cat?▾
Use the pointer or trackpad to aim and set the shot, then judge force, wall rebounds, hazards, and the cat stopping position.
What is the best first-shot strategy?▾
Read the entire hole and choose a safe landing zone that creates a clear second shot instead of forcing a risky hole-in-one attempt.
Can I use walls for bank shots?▾
Yes. Broad flat walls are often the most predictable way to enter a blocked corridor or avoid a direct hazard.
Does Uncanny Cat Golf work on mobile?▾
It may run in a modern mobile browser, but landscape orientation or desktop fullscreen makes aiming and narrow obstacles easier to judge.
Why is the game frame blank or unresponsive?▾
Wait for remote assets, click inside the iframe to restore focus, refresh once, and check extensions or networks that block scripts and embedded games.
Will my best scores remain saved?▾
Save behavior may depend on local browser storage, so clearing site data, changing profiles, or using private mode can remove progress.
Is Uncanny Cat Golf a horror game?▾
It is primarily surreal comedy mini golf. It uses uncanny faces, flashing meme-style imagery, and intentionally strange humor rather than graphic horror in the supplied artwork.
