01

Overview

A free online dark narrative horror game about quarantine, survival, and moral collapse.

The Coffin of Andy and Leyley is a dark narrative horror game developed by Kit9 Studio. Set during a mysterious quarantine, the game follows siblings Andy and Leyley after their parents vanish without explanation. Food deliveries stop, communication dies, and parasite-infested water turns the outside world into a threat, forcing the two to rely on each other to survive no matter the cost.

The game blends dark humor with real survival tension, creating a mood where every choice matters. As conditions worsen, occult elements push the story into more disturbing territory. How It Plays

Exploration, dialogue, and resource decisions drive the free online horror experience.

The Coffin of Andy and Leyley is built around exploration, dialogue, and decision-making. You search the apartment and nearby spaces, inspect bills, diaries, photographs, and torn documents, and slowly piece together what happened to the siblings' parents. Every object helps deepen the atmosphere and makes the free online mystery feel denser and more personal.
