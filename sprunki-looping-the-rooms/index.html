About Sprunki Looping the Rooms

Sprunki Looping the Rooms is a unique Incredibox-style music mod created by community developer GE-WZY on the Cocrea platform. Inspired by the viral Vocaloid track "Looping the Rooms" (rusino featuring Hatsune Miku), it drops the familiar Sprunki cast into a liminal Backrooms nightmare where looping the beat feels like survival.

The core gameplay is intuitive: drag icons onto gray silhouettes to reveal their corrupted forms and add new stems to a driving loop (around 160 BPM). Expect heavy, echoing basslines, distorted synths, frantic percussion, and glitchy vocals that match the oppressive hum of endless fluorescent hallways.

Certain combinations can shift the experience into a hidden horror mode: the yellow walls collapse into darkness or red static, the screen trembles, and the mix becomes heavier and more chaotic. Keep the loop going, experiment boldly, and let the rooms change around your sound.

The Story Behind Sprunki Looping the Rooms

In this mod, the Sprunki cast has "no-clipped" out of their colorful world and fallen into the infinite corridors of the Backrooms. The monotone yellow wallpaper, damp carpet, and relentless buzzing lights set the tone for a psychological descent where the only comfort is rhythm.

As the rooms stretch on, the lore suggests the liminal emptiness warps the characters into anomalous versions of themselves. The beat becomes a lifeline: keep the music looping to hold the darkness back—if the loop stops, the rooms claim what's left.
