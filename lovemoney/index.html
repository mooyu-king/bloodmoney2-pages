Core Game Features

📖 Narrative-Driven Clicking

Experience LoveMoney's unique blend of incremental gameplay and storytelling. Every click advances both your earnings and the compelling narrative, creating an engaging experience unlike any other clicker game.

⚖️ Moral Choice System

LoveMoney presents provocative decisions that test your ethics and challenge your values. Each choice impacts not just your earnings but also determines which of the multiple endings you'll unlock.

🚀 Strategic Upgrade Path

Progress through LoveMoney by purchasing items that multiply your earnings exponentially. Plan your upgrade strategy carefully to reach the crucial $25,000 goal before time runs out.

🎭 Multiple Endings

Your decisions in LoveMoney shape the story's outcome in meaningful ways. Discover various endings based on your moral choices and the path you take to reach your financial goal.

About Love Money

LoveMoney is a revolutionary story-driven clicker RPG that perfectly combines fast-paced incremental gameplay with thought-provoking moral choices. In this unique gaming world, desperate times call for desperate measures.

🎮 Innovative Gameplay

Starting with $1 per click, you must earn $25,000 within limited time for a critical medical procedure. But in LoveMoney, the path to financial salvation is filled with moral compromises and unexpected romantic encounters.

⚖️ Moral Choices

The game's core lies in making players question their choices. Every upgrade and click brings new ethical dilemmas. Will you maintain your integrity, or let desperation push you beyond moral boundaries?

📖 Deep Narrative

Interactions with the complex character Harvey shape the game's narrative. Your decisions don't just affect scores – they fundamentally alter story progression, leading to multiple unique endings.

🎨 Pixel Art

Experience this battle between morality and money in a beautiful pixel art world. Purchase powerful upgrades to boost earnings while facing difficult choices that test humanity.
