About Ormblok

Ormblok stands out in the puzzle game genre by completely reimagining how we interact with falling blocks. Created by Ben Jelter and Mike Leisz for GB Compo 2025, this innovative title flips traditional Tetris logic on its head. Instead of clearing lines, Ormblok challenges you to strategically create gaps that transform into pellets for your worm to consume.

The genius of Ormblok lies in its dual-phase gameplay system. During the block phase, you arrange falling pieces to create strategic 2x2 gaps in the playfield. Once you've built your arena, the game seamlessly transitions to Worm Mode, where you control a snake-like creature navigating through your creation to eat pellets.

🎮 Dual-Phase Gameplay

  • • Block Phase: Drop and arrange Tetris-style pieces
  • • Worm Mode: Navigate snake through created gaps
  • • Strategic Planning: Create 2x2 gaps for pellet generation
  • • Risk-Reward System: Complex patterns yield higher scores

🎯 Game Features

  • • Game Boy Color Style: Nostalgic pixel art aesthetic
  • • Progressive Difficulty: Increasing target goals each round
  • • Strategic Depth: Every block placement matters
  • • Accessible Controls: Easy to learn, hard to master
