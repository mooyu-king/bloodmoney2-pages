About Sprunki Looping the Rooms

If you have ever felt the unsettling dread of endless, empty hallways, then Sprunki Looping the Rooms is the music game you need to play. Created by the community developer GE-WZY on the Cocrea platform, this Incredibox-style mod takes the familiar Sprunki cast and traps them in a never-ending liminal nightmare.

Inspired by the viral Vocaloid track "Looping the Rooms" (also known as "Loop the Room") by the Japanese producer rusino featuring Hatsune Miku, the experience blends rhythmic beat-making with the eerie, analog-horror aesthetic of the infinite Backrooms.

The Lore: Trapped in a Liminal Nightmare

The mod thrusts its cast into Level 0 of the Backrooms: monotonous yellow wallpaper, damp carpet, and the maddening hum of fluorescent lights. The lore implies that the emptiness infects their minds, twisting them into anomalous versions of themselves as they wander identical corridors.

The beat becomes a lifeline. The only way to keep the encroaching darkness at bay is to keep the music looping. If the loop stops, the rooms claim them forever.

Gameplay Mechanics: How to Play in the Backrooms

  • Start on a yellow Backrooms hallway stage with gray, featureless silhouettes and constant ambient buzzing.
  • Drag icons onto silhouettes to reveal corrupted forms and add audio stems to a fast loop (around 160 BPM).
  • Layer echoing bass, distorted synth melodies, frantic beatboxing percussion, and glitchy vocals.
  • Discover specific combinations that trigger a hidden horror mode: darkness or red static, screen vibration, and heavier industrial rhythms.
