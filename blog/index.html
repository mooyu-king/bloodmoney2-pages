FAQ

Q: Is Blood Money 2 suitable for all ages?

A: Blood Money 2 contains mature themes related to corporate ethics and dark humor. It's recommended for players 16 and older.

Q: How long does it take to complete the game?

A: The main story takes approximately 8-12 hours to complete, with additional content available for extended gameplay.

Q: Are there multiple difficulty levels?

A: Yes, the game offers three difficulty levels: Executive, Manager, and CEO, each with different challenges and complexity.

Q: Can I play offline?

A: Yes, Blood Money 2 can be played entirely offline. Online features are optional and include leaderboards and community sharing.

Q: Will there be DLC or expansions?

A: We're currently working on additional content based on community feedback. Stay tuned for announcements!
