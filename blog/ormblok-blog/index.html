🎮 What is OrmBlok?

OrmBlok is a strategic block puzzle game that combines the classic appeal of Tetris-style gameplay with modern innovations. Players must strategically place falling blocks to create complete lines while managing special power-ups and obstacles.

Unlike traditional block games, OrmBlok introduces unique mechanics like block transformation, combo multipliers, and strategic power-ups that add layers of depth to every move you make.

🧠 Strategy Guide

🎯 Basic Strategies

  • •Always keep the center columns clear for emergency placements
  • •Plan 2-3 moves ahead to avoid getting trapped
  • •Save power-ups for critical moments when you're close to the top
  • •Focus on creating multiple line clears simultaneously for bonus points

⚡ Advanced Tips

  • •Master the T-spin technique for maximum point efficiency
  • •Use the hold feature strategically to set up perfect placements
  • •Learn block rotation patterns to maximize placement options
  • •Combine power-ups for devastating combo effects
