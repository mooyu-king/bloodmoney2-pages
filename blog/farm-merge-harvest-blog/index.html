About Farm: Merge Harvest

Overview

Begin with simple resources and use drag-and-merge to create higher-tier crops and tools. Gradually expand planting, production, and storage zones, keeping a gentle pace for cozy growth.

Core Mechanics

  • Merge upgrades: combine identical items to improve quality and yield, building efficient production lines.
  • Resource loop: complete the cycle of sowing, watering, harvesting, processing, and selling to maintain cash flow.
  • Layout planning: grid-based areas allow free placement and smooth flow from farming to processing.

Progress and Rewards

  • Unlock new crops, tools, and advanced buildings to expand playable content.
  • Complete daily/stage tasks and claim rewards to accelerate growth.
  • Visual growth curve: income, capacity, and efficiency improve steadily through merges.

Strategy and Unlocks

  • Prioritize key nodes: establish core lines and supporting facilities first to secure cash flow and supply.
  • Batch merges: avoid blind expansion; accumulate low-tier items and merge in batches for better efficiency.
  • Zone management: define farming, processing, and storage zones clearly to reduce drag distance and wait time.

Building and Design

  • Upgrade buildings: towers, mills, and warehouses support tiered upgrades for capacity and efficiency gains.
  • Flow optimization: place frequent actions along a central axis to reduce cross-zone movement.
  • Unified theme: greenery and wood tones convey a cozy countryside atmosphere.

Visual Feedback

  • Clean merge effects highlight the satisfaction of growth milestones.
  • Readable state cues: maturity, capacity, and stock shown via icons and color.
  • Performance-friendly: smooth interactions and animations even on modest hardware.

Controls

  • Drag with mouse to merge; click to harvest, upgrade, and purchase.
  • Shortcut support and common action optimizations reduce repetitive tasks.
  • Gradual onboarding lowers early complexity for new players.

Tip: Prioritize a stable main production line, then expand layout and polish.
