About BloodMoney 2

Welcome to BloodMoney 2, where the Human Expenditure Program transforms life management into dark comedy gold. BloodMoney 2 revolutionizes the simulation genre by putting you in charge of Harvey Harvington's existence in this satirical simulation that questions the value of human life in corporate America.

The Human Expenditure Program mechanics in BloodMoney 2 go beyond simple resource management, incorporating complex systems where each decision impacts Harvey's physical health, mental state, and financial situation. BloodMoney 2 challenges players to navigate difficult choices: will you prioritize Harvey's wellbeing or maximize corporate profits?

What makes BloodMoney 2 special is its unflinching satire of modern capitalism through the Human Expenditure Program. BloodMoney 2 presents a dystopian world where corporations literally own their employees, and you're the middle manager deciding Harvey's fate. Through dark humor and strategic gameplay, BloodMoney 2 forces players to confront uncomfortable truths about corporate culture and human value.

Development History

2019 - The Original Blood Money

The first Blood Money game was released as an indie project, gaining a cult following for its unique take on corporate satire and dark humor.

2021 - Community Growth

The game's community grew significantly, with players sharing strategies, discussing the game's social commentary, and creating fan content.

2023 - Development Begins

Development of Blood Money 2: Human Expenditure Program officially began, incorporating extensive community feedback and expanding on the original's themes.

2024 - Release

Blood Money 2 launches with enhanced graphics, deeper gameplay mechanics, expanded narrative, and the innovative Human Expenditure Program system.
