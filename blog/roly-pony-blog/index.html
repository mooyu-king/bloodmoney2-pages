About Roly Pony

Roly Pony is a delightful horse-themed puzzle game that brings the beloved Suika-style gameplay to your browser. In this charming adventure, you'll combine adorable horses and feeds to unlock new characters while managing your pasture space.

The game features intuitive drag-and-drop mechanics where you strategically place horses and feed items in your pasture. When identical items touch, they merge to create new, more valuable characters and items, creating a satisfying progression system.

With its colorful graphics, charming horse characters, and relaxing gameplay, Roly Pony offers a perfect blend of strategy and casual fun that appeals to players of all ages.

"Roly Pony combines the addictive merging mechanics of Suika games with adorable horse characters, creating a charming puzzle experience that's both relaxing and engaging. Perfect for casual gaming sessions or extended play."
