About Home Is Where He Is

Home Is Where He Is is a psychological horror game that you can play directly in your browser. Home Is Where He Is takes a simple premise—returning to a place you once called home—and turns it into a deeply unsettling experience where nothing is quite as you remember it. Home Is Where He Is draws you into a slow-burn narrative where the familiar walls of a childhood house start to feel like the walls of a prison.

Home Is Where He Is is built around atmosphere rather than jump scares. The horror in Home Is Where He Is comes from small, creeping details: a door that should not be open, a voice that sounds almost right but not quite, a photograph on the wall where someone is standing just a little too close. Home Is Where He Is rewards patience and observation, not speed. The longer you stay, the more Home Is Where He Is reveals about why you left—and why coming back was a mistake.

Because Home Is Where He Is runs entirely in the browser, there is no download to wait for and no installation to manage. You can start Home Is Where He Is right now and let the story pull you into its world. Home Is Where He Is is built for players who love narrative horror, psychological tension, and stories that stay with you long after the browser tab closes.

In Home Is Where He Is, you play as someone who has been away for a long time. The reasons for your absence are unclear at first, and Home Is Where He Is does not rush to explain them.
